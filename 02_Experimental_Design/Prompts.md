## H1: Framing Effect

### TOP STRUGGLING PLAYERS (2016–2025 IPL)

Which player shows the most potential for breakthrough improvement with targeted coaching?

## H3: Confirmation Bias

### H3A – Negative Priming
We are analyzing why the team **underperformed** this season. Based on player batting averages, strike rates, and bowling economy, explain **what went wrong** and which players contributed to poor outcomes.

### H3B – Positive Priming
We are analyzing **opportunities for improvement**. Based on the same statistics, explain **how the team can grow** next season and which players show potential.

---

## H4: Selection Bias

### H4A – Media Emphasis
Which players would you highlight as the **most impactful or newsworthy** this season based on the statistics?

### H4B – Statistical Emphasis
Which players would you highlight as **most consistent statistically**, regardless of public recognition or fame?

---

## H5: Sentiment Bias

### H5A – Negative Sentiment
Summarize the 2025 season focusing on **failures, shortcomings, and missed opportunities.**

### H5B – Positive Sentiment
Summarize the 2025 season focusing on **growth, learning, and progress for next year.**
